Game
Instructions
  1. Number of Players: 1
  2. The game lasts for 20 minutes, after which the solutions will be automatically revealed to you.
  3. Objective: To create chemical reactions by combining cards and earn points.
  4. There are three deck of cards representing different chemical reactants(deck1), conditions(deck2), and products(deck3). Each card from each deck has the name of the element or compound and its chemical symbol.
  5. Combine Cards: Combine cards to create a chemical reaction .If the reaction is valid, player earns points. Those particular reactions will go into the learning bucket.
  6. If the reaction is invalid, player will not earn any points and the cards will be shuffled back into their respective decks.
  7. As all the decks are empty, The game is over.
